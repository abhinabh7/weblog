A Journey to Mardi Himal, Nepal

20th Oct 2023

Trekking through the enchanting landscapes of Mardi Himal in Nepal is a journey like no other, offering a blend of stunning natural beauty and rich cultural experiences. Starting from Pokhara, our adventure unfolds with a drive to Phedi, leading us through lush forests, charming villages, and cascading waterfalls. As we ascend to High Camp, the rugged terrain and crisp air invigorate our spirits, culminating in a breathtaking sunset over the Annapurna range. The trek to Mardi Himal Base Camp presents its challenges, but the sight of Machapuchare towering above us is a reward in itself. The final push to the summit is tough, but the panoramic views of the Annapurna range from the top make every step worth it. This journey is not just about conquering peaks; it's about discovering the magic of the Himalayas, the warmth of the Nepalese people, and the joy of trekking. As we make our way back, we carry with us memories of a lifetime, a deep appreciation for nature's wonders, and a longing for more adventures in the future.

My Journey on the Football Field

27th Oct 2020

Stepping onto the football field is an experience like no other. Before the match, the locker room buzzed with excitement and focus. The coach's pep talk fueled our determination, and we stepped onto the field ready to give it our all. The whistle blew, signaling the start of the match. The ball was at my feet, and I felt a surge of energy as I dribbled past defenders, looking for an opening. The teamwork was crucial as we passed the ball seamlessly, each player knowing their role. As the game progressed, the intensity grew. Every tackle, every pass, and every shot mattered. The crowd's cheers were like music to my ears, pushing me to perform my best. Whether we won or lost, the experience of playing football is always rewarding. The game taught me the value of teamwork, perseverance, and the joy of pursuing a passion.
